# Analog Understanding

## Goal
Develop intuitive understanding of analog circuits and ability to design them.

## Motivation
- Current gap in knowledge compared to digital/embedded
- Desire to move beyond "it works" to "I understand why it works"

## Pain Points
- Math-heavy topics (filters, signal processing)
- Lack of intuitive mental models

## Strategy
- Focus on intuition first, math second
- Build and test simple circuits
- Use simulation + real measurements

## Notes
Analog is a long-term skill, not quick win. Needs iterative exposure.
