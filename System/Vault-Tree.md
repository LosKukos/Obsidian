# Vault Structure

## Core folders

### System/
Rules and structural definitions.

### Inbox/
Temporary capture for things not yet sorted.

### Identity/
Stable information about the user.
- Profile.md
- Preferences.md

### Projects/
- Active/
- Archived/

### Knowledge/
Topic-based knowledge.
Examples:
- Biomedical/
- Electronics/
- IT/

### People/
Important people and relationships.

### Logs/
- Daily/
- Session-Summaries/

### MOCs/
Map of Content notes (navigation layer).

### Templates/
Reusable note structures.

### Attachments/
Files and media.

## Design philosophy
- Flat enough to stay usable
- Structured enough to scale
- Everything should have a clear place
